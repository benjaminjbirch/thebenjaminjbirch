He came looking for rescue. The alien who found him had other ideas.

When scientist Dean Carver is stranded on a violent alien moon, survival arrives in the form of Koren: enormous, fiercely protective, and absolutely certain Dean belongs with him.

A broken translator turns devotion into possession, rescue into abduction, and first contact into a battle over one dangerous word: mine. But as military forces close in, Dean must decide where duty ends—and where desire begins.
